Rationale of the Study

In this study, the researchers will develop a system that will help people to park their car in the available area. The invention provides a parking area for a car, a device will detect for a car if there is an available space. If a car is in the slotted parking area, the sensor information is translated to parking space counts. If a car is in the unslotted parking area, the sensor information is translated to a parking space map. The monitor will show the necessary information to the driver. The driver will know if there's an available space in parking area or not.
